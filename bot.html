Act 1: Genesis of Radiants


First Light

The First Light is the catalyst for everything in the Valorant universe. Taking place in an alternate Earth, a mysterious event occurs, unleashing Radianite, a powerful and unknown energy source. This energy brings rapid technological advances but also causes global instability. People affected by Radianite develop unique powers, becoming known as Radiants. Tensions rise between ordinary humans and Radiants, leading to conflict.

To protect the world from misuse of Radianite, an organization known as the Valorant Protocol is formed. The Protocol recruits agents from around the world, many of whom have Radiant abilities. This elite group is tasked with maintaining global security and understanding the mysterious power that changed the world.


The Rise of Radiants

As the Valorant Protocol gains strength, they realize that Radianite isn’t just an energy source but can also be weaponized. The agents begin investigating Radianite sites and discover that there’s a shadowy organization (later revealed to be Kingdom Corporation) controlling large quantities of Radianite. Kingdom Corporation claims to harness Radianite for the betterment of humanity, but their methods are questionable.

New agents, both Radiants and non-Radiants, join the ranks of Valorant to fight this growing threat. However, some Radiants are seen as dangerous, and governments worldwide grow more distrustful of them. Meanwhile, the Protocol starts using spike technology to control the Radianite, leading to skirmishes in key locations around the globe.



Act 2: Dimensional Reckoning


Mirror Earth Invasion

In a shocking twist, the agents discover that they aren’t just battling for control of Radianite on their own Earth. They are being invaded by agents from Mirror Earth, an alternate dimension where everything is a dark reflection of their own reality. In this Mirror Earth, resources have become scarce due to Radianite depletion, and Mirror Agents are sent to extract Radianite from Earth Prime to save their world.

The appearance of Mirror Agents shocks the Valorant Protocol, as they come face-to-face with alternate versions of themselves. This arc delves into the mystery of Mirror Earth and how both dimensions are linked by Radianite. As battles ensue, the agents from both dimensions clash, each fighting to protect their own world’s interests.


Kingdom's Gambit

As the battle with Mirror Earth intensifies, the true nature of Kingdom Corporation comes to light. Kingdom isn’t just a multinational organization—it's deeply intertwined with the events that led to the First Light and is secretly manipulating both Earth Prime and Mirror Earth. It turns out that Kingdom's ultimate goal is to control Radianite across all dimensions and become the dominant force in the multiverse.

Valorant agents now find themselves fighting a two-front war: stopping Kingdom’s global domination while also fending off Mirror Earth agents who grow increasingly desperate. The stakes are raised as agents discover that if one Earth is destroyed, the balance between dimensions could collapse, destroying both worlds.



Act 3: Final Frontiers


Duelists: The Frontline

This arc focuses on the Duelists, a group of Valorant agents who are at the forefront of the fight against Kingdom and Mirror Earth. The agents are divided in their tactics, with some favoring more aggressive strategies, while others push for diplomacy with Mirror Earth.

During this arc, the Duelists take center stage, facing off against powerful enemies from both dimensions. The personal stories of characters like Phoenix, Jett, and Reyna are explored as they confront not only external threats but also internal doubts and betrayals.


The Future Unknown

With Mirror Earth increasingly unstable and Kingdom Corporation enacting its final plan, the future of both dimensions hangs in the balance. Valorant agents are forced to make impossible choices that will determine the fate of their world—and possibly all worlds. New alliances form, old enemies resurface, and the battle reaches its climax in a global conflict that could reshape reality itself.

This arc sets the stage for the next chapter in the Valorant universe, as agents must face the consequences of their actions and a potentially even larger cosmic threat looming on the horizon.

Ascent // San Marco, Venice, Veneto, Italy // 45°26'BF" N 12°20'Q" E

An open playground for small wars of position and attrition divide two sites on Ascent. Each site can be fortified by irreversible bomb doors; once they’re down, you’ll have to destroy them or find another way. Yield as little territory as possible.

Bind // Rabat, Rabat-Salé-Kénitra, Morocco // 34°2'A" N 6°51'Z" W

Two sites. No middle. Gotta pick left or right. What’s it going to be then? Both offer direct paths for attackers and a pair of one-way teleporters make it easier to flank.

Haven // Thimphu, Thimphu District, Bhutan // 27°28'A" N 89°38'WZ" E

Beneath a forgotten monastery, a clamour emerges from rival Agents clashing to control three sites. There’s more territory to control, but defenders can use the extra real estate for aggressive pushes.

Split // Shinjuku, Tokyo, Kantō, Japan // 35°41'CD" N 139°41'WX" E

If you want to go far, you’ll have to go up. A pair of sites split by an elevated center allows for rapid movement using two rope ascenders. Each site is built with a looming tower vital for control. Remember to watch above before it all blows sky-high.
